Building Agentic Systems
Design tension

Autonomy vs. control

More autonomy means fewer hand-offs, faster resolution, and emergent problem-solving — but a wider blast radius when the model is wrong.

More control means deterministic gates, sandboxes, and human-in-the-loop checkpoints — predictable, auditable, and slower.
